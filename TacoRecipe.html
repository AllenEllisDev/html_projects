Easy Street Tacos

A quick, at-home recipe for delicious street tacos!

Ingredients

  • Small Tortillas
  • Round Steak or Ground Beef(1lb)
  • Taco Seasoning
  • Limes(x2)
  • Pico De Gallo
  • Hot Sauce

Instructions

  1. Slice round steak into small 1/4 inch cubes(move to step 2 if using ground beef)
  2. Add taco seasoning to chopped steak/beef
  3. Brown beef in skillet on high
  4. Remove beef from skillet and add to tortillas
  5. Add one scoop of Pico De Gallo
  6. Top with squeezed lime slices
  7. Enjoy! :)
